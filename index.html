About learning to crochet with Cripulette

Hello and Welcome! I am Cripulette, a 40 years old graphic designer with a passion for crafting. I have been doing crochet since I am a Kid and teaching it for the past 10 years. I take pride of seeing my students creating the art object of their dreams. My method to teach is simple and efficient: Simple explanations, sharing idea with other students and lot of positive feed back to keep the motivation up. My courses are for beginner and intermediate crochet enthusiasts. For the more advance 'crochetists', you are welcome to my workshops that offer to practice on theme. The workshops are for all levels and because I keep it small (maximum 6 people) the explanation are adapted to each level. Interested? Look at the courses and workshops I offer and then send me message to book your place.

4 good reasons to learn crochet

Imagination

Just by imagining your next creation and looking at others work, crochet will stimulate your brain and stimulate your imagination

Creation

As each art work is a creation, crochet will make you happy by the multitude of creations you will make

Relaxation

The repetitive movement Crochet is relaxing can help to achieve mindfulness, the state of being fully immersed in the present moment

Enjoyment

Crochet leads to many types of enjoyment. The pleasure of doing it, the result of an art creation and the Joy of offering it to the people you love
