About learning to Crochet with Cripulette

Hello and Welcome! I am Cripulette, a 40 year old architect with a passion for crafts. I have been crocheting since I was a child and have been teaching it for 10 years. I take pride in seeing my students create the art object of their dreams. My teaching method is simple and effective: Simple explanations, sharing ideas with other students and lots of positive feedback to keep the motivation going. My classes are for beginners and intermediate crochet enthusiasts. For the more advanced 'crocheters', you are welcome to join my workshops which offer practice on a theme. The workshops are for all levels and as I keep the class small (6 people maximum), the explanations are adapted to each level. Are you interested in learning crochet? Just have a look at the courses and workshops I offer.

4 good reasons to learn crochet

Imagination

Just by imagining your next creation and looking at the work of others, crochet will stimulate your brain and your imagination

Creation

As each artwork is a creation, crochet will make you happy by the multitude of creations you will make

Relaxation

The repetitive movement crochet is relaxing and can help to achieve full consciousness, a state of total immersion in the present moment

Enjoyment

Crocheting brings many types of pleasure. The pleasure of doing it, the result of an artistic creation and the joy of offering it to the people you love
