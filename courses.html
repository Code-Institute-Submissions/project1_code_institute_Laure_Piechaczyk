Weekly courses

Beginner course

Every Monday 6 - 8 pm

From September the 6th to December the 20th 2021

This course is for you if you are interested in learning the basics of crochet. It focuses on basic crochet stitches, tools and takes you through your project.

Price and details

What should you bring?

Whatever you have. If you have crochets and wool, bring them. If not, you can bring nothing and use the crochets and wool we have to learn the stitches. However, for your projects you will need your own wool but we can discuss this before you buy it.

Registration

No registration is necessary for this course, just come along to 99 Royal Park Terrace, Edinburgh. If you have any questions, please email cripulettecrochet@gmail.com or post a message here.

Price

10 £ per class, you can choose to come once or every week.

Intermediate course

This course is for you if you have already some knowledge in crochet and want to expand it. It focuses on the technics and takes you through your project.

Workshops

Keep your inner child alive

25th & 26th September

10am to 4pm

As a gift or for yourself, there is always a good reason to make toys. This workshop focuses on the creation of toys and the specific techniques for making them. It is suitable for all levels and takes place in small groups of maximum 6 people.

Price and details

Warm Winter

23rd & 24th October

10am to 4pm

Winter is coming! Stay warm with a collection of hats, scarves, jumpers ... This workshop focuses on the creation of woollen clothing for adults and the specific techniques for making them. It is suitable for all levels and takes place in a small group of maximum 6 people.

Price and details

Dress your littel friend

20th & 21th November

10am to 4pm

Homemade clothes for babies and children are really cute, aren't they? This workshop focuses on creating clothes for babies & children and the specific techniques for making them. It is suitable for all levels and takes place in a small group of maximum 6 people.

Price and details

Workshop details

What should you bring?

Your own material. You can try out the crochets we have but, as a tip, use your own material. We can of course discuss the new material before you buy it.

Registration

The maximum group size is 6 people. To reserve your place, please send a message to cripulettecrochet@gmail.com or a message here.

Organisation

Workshops start at 10am with an introduction and Saturday morning is devoted to discussing personal projects. From Saturday afternoon until Sunday afternoon, the techniques adapted to your projects are shown and you can start your own project. For lunch, we usually eat on site or in the park if the weather is nice. You can bring your own food, we have a microwave and cutlery.

Price

85£ for the weekend
