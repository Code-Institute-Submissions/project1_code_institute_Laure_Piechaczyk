Weekly courses

Beginner course

Every Monday 6 - 8 pm

From September the 9th to december the 23th 2021

This course is for you if you are interested in learning the basics of crochet. It focus on basic crochet stitches, tools and takes you through your project.

Price and details

What to bring?

Whatever you have. If you have crochets and yarn, come over with. Otherwise you can bring nothing and use the crochets and yarns we have to learn stitches. However for you projects, you will need to have your own wool but we can discuss before you buy it.

Registration

No registration is required for this course, just come to 99 Royal Park Terrace, Edinburgh. If you have any question, please send a message to cripulettecrochet@gmail.com or a message here .

Price

10 £ each course, You can choose to come once or every week.
